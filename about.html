DayZ Reforged - About

DayZ Reforged is a gaming community, built on Arma Reforger using a series of mods to give a very unique gameplay, DayZ but reimagined on next gen consoles and the PC using Arma Reforger and the Enfusion Engine.

DayZ and Arma Reforger are both released by Bohemia (Bohemia Interactive Studio, BOHEMIA INTERACTIVE a.s.) however this server takes the best of both worlds, the epic survival nature of DayZ and the beautiful graphics and modern physics in the Arma Reforger game, creating what is effectively just short of DayZ II.

This website is aimed at providing support to new comers on the site and provide knowledge base support, tips, tricks and how-to's.

At the moment, this website is not affiliated with DayZ Reforged directly and is a fan-site created by gamers that love this server so much that they want it to succeed, and are so inspired by the Admins and Modders of this server community, that they want to help it succeed.
